Mumbai holds the second-largest collection of Art Deco buildings in the world after Miami. This rich architectural heritage is now experiencing a renaissance in interior design, reinterpreted for contemporary living spaces.

## Geometric Elegance

Indian Art Deco is characterized by its bold geometry—stepped forms, sunbursts, and chevron patterns—often infused with local motifs. To bring this into your home, look for wallpapers or rugs featuring these strong, structured designs.

## Luxurious Materials

The era was defined by its use of opulent materials. Incorporate high-gloss wood finishes, exotic veneers, and metallic accents in brass or chrome. Terrazzo, a staple of Indian flooring, also has strong ties to the Art Deco movement and is making a huge comeback.

## Color Palettes

Move beyond the stark monochrome of international Deco. Indian Art Deco embraces color: deep jewel tones like emerald green, sapphire blue, and rich ruby red evoke the glamour of the 1930s while feeling distinctly warm and welcoming.

## Balancing the Look

The key to a successful modern Deco interior is restraint. Use Deco elements as accents—a distinctive light fixture, a geometric console table, or a statement armchair—against a cleaner, more contemporary backdrop to prevent the space from feeling like a museum exhibit.
